Career Objective

To work as an economist with an innovative company where I can contribute my ideas, knowledge, and skills to help execute meaningful projects.

A Brief History

I have recently earned a Masters in Economics from the University of California, Irvine. Before this, I earned a Bachelors and Masters in Economics from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. During this time, I wrote a Master's thesis that examines how families change their charitable giving behavior in response to charitable donations made by their reference group members. I have also completed two professional internships. First, I helped to implement an enterprise software system for Kimbrell's Furniture. Second, I found cost saving vendor alternatives in the purchasing department of the University of North Carolina at Charlotte.
